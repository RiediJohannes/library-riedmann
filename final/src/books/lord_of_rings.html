Lord of Rings

Synopsis

Frodo Baggins, a hobbit tasked with destroying the One Ring, a powerful artifact created by the dark lord Sauron to dominate the world. Frodo is joined by a diverse fellowship of companions, including humans, dwarves, elves, and other hobbits, as they journey through dangerous lands to Mount Doom, where the Ring must be destroyed. The trilogy explores themes of friendship, courage, sacrifice, and the struggle against one's own darkness. It consists of the books "The Fellowship of the Ring," "The Two Towers," and "The Return of the King." The series has become a landmark in fantasy literature and was successfully adapted into a film trilogy by Peter Jackson.
